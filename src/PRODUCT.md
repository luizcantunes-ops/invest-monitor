# Investment Monitor — Product Context

## Users
Single user: Luiz Cesar Antunes. Sophisticated private investor managing ~$1.26M USD across US equities (Charles Schwab) and Brazilian funds/FIIs/crypto (Itaú Personnalité). Not a day trader. Makes deliberate, researched decisions.

## Product Purpose
Personal portfolio intelligence dashboard. Monitors US equities (long-term and swing), Brazilian assets (FIIs, crypto via HASH11), macro indicators, and surfaces swing trade candidates from external screener. Sends alerts via Telegram bot.

## Register
product

## Tone
Calm authority. Like a premium private banking tool, not a crypto terminal or retail trading app. Data-dense but not cluttered. No hype, no decorative alerts.

## Anti-references
- Crypto trading terminals (Binance, Bybit UI)
- Generic SaaS dashboards with hero metrics
- Dark neon finance apps
- Robinhood-style gamified UI

## Strategic principles
- Information earns its place. No decorative elements.
- Calm confidence. Warnings are measured, not alarming.
- The tool disappears into the decision-making process.
